Our History

At Garden Kalan-an, our story is woven with passion, tradition, and a love for Filipino cuisine. Founded in 2010, our restaurant has become a cherished destination for those seeking authentic flavors and a warm dining experience.


The journey of Garden Kalan-an began when Vanhe Mendoza, a passionate foodie and culinary enthusiast, decided to share the rich and diverse flavors of Filipino cuisine with the community. After years of perfecting family recipes and exploring local ingredients, she opened the doors to Garden Kalan-an, a place where every dish tells a story.


Since our humble beginnings, we have achieved numerous milestones that reflect our commitment to quality and service. In 2015, we received so many good feedback about our Restaurant, which encouraged us to continue our mission of providing exceptional dining experiences. Over the years, we have expanded our menu, introduced seasonal specials, and hosted community events that celebrate Filipino culture.


Garden Kalan-an is not just a restaurant; it is a gathering place for families and friends. We believe in supporting our local community by sourcing fresh ingredients from nearby farms and collaborating with local artisans. Our commitment to sustainability and community engagement is at the heart of everything we do.


As we look to the future, our goal remains the same: to create memorable dining experiences that celebrate the richness of Filipino cuisine. We invite you to join us on this culinary journey and experience the flavors that have made Garden Kalan-an a beloved destination for food lovers.
